# Requirement ID: FR1
- Description: The app shall maintain a stable connection to ensure uninterrupted meditation sessions.
- Source Persona: Frustrated Meditator (P1)
- Traceability: G1
- Acceptance Criteria: Given the user is in a meditation session, When the app encounters a problem, Then it shall not abruptly stop or crash, and shall instead display an error message or pause the session with a clear indication of the issue.

# Requirement ID: FR2
- Description: The app shall respond quickly to user inputs to facilitate a smooth meditation experience.
- Source Persona: Frustrated Meditator (P1)
- Traceability: G1
- Acceptance Criteria: Given the user is navigating through the app, When they interact with a feature or button, Then the app shall respond within a reasonable time frame (less than 2 seconds), and shall not display noticeable delays or unresponsiveness.

# Requirement ID: FR3
- Description: The system shall provide transparent and easy subscription management to prevent unintended auto-renewal.
- Source Persona: Frustrated Health and Wellness User (P2)
- Traceability: G2
- Acceptance Criteria: 
Given a user has an active subscription, 
When they access their account settings, 
Then they can easily view, update, and cancel their subscription plan.

# Requirement ID: FR4
- Description: The system shall provide clear communication and support to users regarding subscription renewal and pricing.
- Source Persona: Frustrated Health and Wellness User (P2)
- Traceability: G2
- Acceptance Criteria: 
Given a user's subscription is approaching renewal, 
When they log in to their account, 
Then they receive a clear notification about the upcoming renewal and pricing details.

# Requirement ID: FR5
- Description: The meditation app shall ensure stable performance to prevent frequent crashes and allow uninterrupted meditation sessions.
- Source Persona: Frustrated Meditator (P3)
- Traceability: G3
- Acceptance Criteria: Given the user is in a meditation session, when the app encounters a technical issue, then it shall automatically recover or provide a seamless way to resume the session without data loss.

# Requirement ID: FR6
- Description: The meditation app shall provide reliable offline functionality to allow users to access meditation content without an internet connection.
- Source Persona: Frustrated Meditator (P3)
- Traceability: G3
- Acceptance Criteria: Given the user has downloaded meditation content for offline use, when they are not connected to the internet, then they shall be able to access and play the downloaded content without any performance issues or loss of functionality.
